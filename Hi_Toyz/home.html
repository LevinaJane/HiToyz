Background History

HI-Toyz is a toy store that has won the hearts of local collectors and enthusiasts since its establishment in 2019. The store's mission is to provide a haven for toy lovers of all ages, offering a wide selection of high quality, unique and popular toys from various brands.

HI-Toyz was founded by a group of toy enthusiasts, this toy store started as a small retail store in a busy neighborhood. The founders realized that the local market lacked a specialty toy store that fulfill the needs of nostalgic and collectible toys for children and adults. The store is designed to evoke a sense of wonder and nostalgia, with carefully selected displays and themed areas that evoke memories of beloved childhood toys. The founders knew how important it was to promote a sense of community among collectors and enthusiasts, not just selling toys.

With HI-Toyz' unique and high quality products, the store quickly gained a loyal following. The store has also become an activity center for toy collectors that provides a wide selection of action figures, puzzles, model kits, board games, plush toys and more. The store became a gathering place for local collectors to mingle, share stories and have friendly discussions about their favorite toys and series. HI-Toyz's success is due to our commitment to customer satisfaction. Passionate and knowledgeable about toys, HI-Toyz staff are always ready to advise and help customers looking for their next treasured item. In addition, the store regularly hosts events such as toy swaps, special guest appearances and themed parties, giving collectors the opportunity to network and grow their collections.

Recognizing the growing demand for online shopping and convenience, HI-Toyz expanded its business with an e-commerce platform. This online store allows collectors from all over the country and the world to access the store's wide range of toys and merchandise. Over the years, HI-Toyz has built strong relationships with toy manufacturers, retailers and local artists. Through these partnerships, the store has been able to secure exclusive merchandise and limited editions, also become an official reseller for big brands such as Tamiya, Bandai, Mattel Toys, and many more.

HI-Toyz remains a treasured local institution and a haven for toy lovers. The company's commitment to providing an excellent customer experience, creating a sense of community, and offering a variety of high quality toys has established itself as a favorite place for collectors of all ages. Whether customers want to relive childhood memories or discover the latest and greatest toys, HI-Toyz remains the ultimate destination for toy lovers in the region and beyond.
